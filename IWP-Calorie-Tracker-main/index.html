What We Do?

Welcome to MacroMate, your go-to app for tracking the nutritional content of meals at all dining halls and restaurants on campus. Whether you're managing macros for fitness, navigating dietary restrictions, or seeking healthier options, MacroMate provides real-time menu updates and detailed nutrition info, including calories, protein, carbs, and fats. Filter meals by dietary preferences or build custom ones to meet your goals. Our mission is to help you stay on track while enjoying the variety of campus dining options.

  • Ice Cream Mile

    Penn State Campus Recreation Competitive and Intramural Sports and Fitness and Wellbeing will be hosting the Campus Recreation Ice Cream Mile to celebrate Penn State and the University community on Saturday, October 12 at 12:00 p.m. Run a lap, eat a cup of ice cream - and repeat for laps 1,2,3 (1 mile). Time is total time – run time and ice cream consumption time. No high-tech chip-timing here. One person with a stopwatch and one with a tablet and a pen. The event will be open to the first 250 Penn State University Park students to register. First 125 are guaranteed T-shirts!
